Cleveland

The Forest City

Cleveland is a Midwestern city known for its stellar football team, beautiful city flower, and an abundance of pristine natural resources In addition to having a great sense of humor about their town, Clevelanders are passionate about sports, dedicated to the arts, open-minded, and incredibly laid back.
